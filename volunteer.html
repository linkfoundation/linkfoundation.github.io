Science Program Volunteer Application

LINK-SCIENCE is a community driven funding program that provides registration grants for children aged 6-12 to participate in sports. Volunteers are needed for organizing activities and assisting the participants at afterschool events at booked locations. This initiative is 100% driven by volunteers and we need your help so all the elementary school students can understand more about their passions.

Responsibilities

  • Set up/take down equipment for after school science clubs
  • Assisting in organising activities for participants
  • Distribution of LINK-Sport information; answering general inquiries about LINK-Sport
  • Assisting the participants in further understanding of interesting material
A Police Information Check is required to become a volunteer for LINK Hobby Discovery.

Language Arts Program Volunteer Application

LINK-LA is a local charity program, a community driven funding program that provides registration grants for children aged 6-12 to participate in Language Arts activities. Volunteers are needed for organizing activities and assisting the participants at afterschool events at booked locations. We believe it is best for children to discover their passions at a relatively young age, therefore we need your help so all elementary students interested can receive further knowledge.

Responsibilities

  • Finding books for the participants
  • Organizing debates among the participants
  • Distribution of LINK-LA information; answering general inquiries about LINK-LA
  • Reading interesting material with the participants
A Police Information Check is required to become a volunteer for LINK Hobby Discovery.

Fine Arts Program Volunteer Application

LINK-Fine Arts is a local charity program, a community driven funding program that provides registration grants for children aged 6-12 to participate in different fine arts clubs. LINK-Fine Arts created 2 clubs, the music and art club, and volunteers are needed for teaching different fine arts skills as well as organizing activities for the participants at afterschool events at booked locations. We have made it our duty to help children recognize their passion at a young age. . Therefore we need your help so all the elementary school students interested can gain a further understanding about fine arts outside of a school environment.

Responsibilities

  • Assisting in organising activities for participants
  • Teaching participants different fine art skills such as sketching cartoon figures
  • Assisting participants when faced with difficulties
  • Distribution of LINK-FA information; answering general inquiries about LINK-FA
A Police Information Check is required to become a volunteer for LINK Hobby Discovery.
