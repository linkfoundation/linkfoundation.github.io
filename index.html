Our Visions

LINK Hobby Discovery Foundation (LINK) aims to provide FREE, high quality educational programs for elementary school students around the Lower Mainland.

Founded by high school students, LINK is a newly formed organization and has been legally certified as a non-profit society under the federal government. LINK believes that it is extremely important for each child to not only become passionate about learning, but also to find joy while exploring the limitless amount of knowledge. They deserve to be given the encouragement and space to try new things, explore new ideas and develop their own unique opinions.

We seek to achieve this goal through our integrated STEM programs funded through partnerships with community level organizations as well as individual fundraising events.
