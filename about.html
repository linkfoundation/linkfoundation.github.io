About Us

Our Story

LINK was founded by high school students eager to make a difference within the education system. A noticeable flaw we realized was when students reach high school; homework takes over and the pursuit of hobbies becomes limited. While advancing through the education system children realize that the workloads will get harder. The problem though is many young children do not care to adhere and adapt to a strenuous workload. The action taken after school hours can greatly influence a student's success academically and socially. Instead of going home and needlessly devoting hours upon hours to YouTube or video games LINK encourages students to become more productive. Through the nurturing of students, we provide the vital first steps into success within and outside of a school atmosphere. The steps to take action and discover something that bears significance to a child. Find a passion.

Our Team

The LINK team is a collective solely run by high school students. It creates an opportunity for students old and young to cooperate and learn from each other. The founders of LINK are Andre Nowak and Paul Lin. Our chief technology officer is Sam Schweigel and other key contributors include Sam-Samida Pugh, Connor Bews and AJ Lemieux. But most of all we would like to recognize and thank all of our industrious volunteers. Without their contributions LINK would never be able to expand and reach out to so many children within the community.
