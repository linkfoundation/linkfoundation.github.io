About Us

Our Story

LINK was founded by high-school students eager to make a difference within the education system. A noticeable flaw we realized was that many high-school students are not necessarily passionate about exploring new knowledge. While advancing through the education system, and especially during the times in high school, students will undoubtedly notice a steady increase in work load. Much of the students become less motivated to accomplish their original goals as they are unable to adhere and adapt to the strenuous workload. We believe this is merely a symptom of a problem that had started way before high-school. The development of work habits and the beginning of hobbies that results in a driven and dedicated attitude towards new knowledge can be traced back to elementary school, where students first encounter formal education. The cause of the problem is simply the actions taken during after-school hours, which can greatly influence a student's success academically and socially. Instead of going home and needlessly devoting hours upon hours to YouTube or video games, LINK encourages students to become more productive. A passion will not only develop effective work habits, but will also provide the students with a reason to seek new knowledge continuously. Through the nurturing of students, we provide the vital first steps into success within and outside of a school atmosphere. The steps to take action and discover something that bears significance to a child. Find a passion.

Goals of LINK for:

Children

The board of directors and volunteers of LINK believes that every child deserves a safe, warm and caring environment, where they can discover their true passions, whether that be in humanities or sciences. We are committed to fostering the children’s confidence, and create programs that will promote their intellectual growth and ￼development. They deserve to be given the encouragement and space to try new things, explore new ideas and develop their own unique view.

Parents

LINK is committed to provide a safe and reliable program for the children, which allows the parents the peace of mind they require to go to work and be worry-free during the times when the students are attending the programs. We believe that it is essential to give parents the opportunities for feedbacks and involvement.

Volunteers & Staffs

To achieve the unified goals of LINK, we are committed to hiring, training and fairly compensating of staff. Most staffs will be volunteers and will usually be high-school students who have a positive and sensitive attitude towards children. Despite not being experts, we believe that high-school students can provide a more modernized view on the specific fields of studies and create an energetic environment in the classroom. 

High School Volunteers

LINK is committed to provide a fantastic learning opportunity, in the areas of programming and administration, to students in high-school. It is a great way to gain experience and pass on the knowledge/positive views you have learnt throughout high-school.

Our Team

LINK was founded by Paul Lin and Andre Nowak over the summer of 2016. The LINK team is solely run by high school students, creating an opportunity for students old and young to cooperate and learn from each other. Each and every one of our staffs/volunteers represents the spirit of our organization: driven, committed, and acutely aware of the limitless potentials of education. We go through in-depth procedures to identify motivated individuals with high-academic skills and social abilities to produce a friendly team that will be a positive influence to all the participants.
